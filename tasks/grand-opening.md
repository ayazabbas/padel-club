# Grand Opening

**Status:** ⏳ Not Started  
**Target:** Late 2026  
**Dependencies:**
- ⏳ Courts installed and tested
- ⏳ All permits and licenses in place
- ⏳ Staff hired and trained
- ⏳ Booking system live
- ⏳ Marketing campaign executed

---

## Objective

Launch the padel club with maximum impact — full bookings from day one.

---

## Pre-Launch Phase (4-6 Weeks Before)

### 1. Final Permits & Compliance
- [ ] Building control sign-off (if applicable)
- [ ] Fire safety inspection
- [ ] Discharge any planning conditions
- [ ] Public liability insurance in place
- [ ] Employer's liability insurance (if hiring)
- [ ] Business rates registration

### 2. Hire & Train Staff
- [ ] Determine staffing needs:
  - Manager (could be a partner initially)
  - Part-time reception/check-in
  - Coaches (freelance or employed)
  - Cleaning
- [ ] Advertise positions (Indeed, local networks)
- [ ] Interview and hire
- [ ] Train on:
  - Booking system
  - Customer service
  - Emergency procedures
  - Opening/closing procedures

### 3. Set Up Operations
- [ ] Booking system live and tested
- [ ] Pricing structure finalized
- [ ] Membership options configured
- [ ] Payment processing working
- [ ] Bank account operational
- [ ] Accounting system set up (Xero, QuickBooks, etc.)

### 4. Marketing Campaign
- [ ] **Website** — Basic site with booking, location, prices
- [ ] **Google My Business** — Claim listing, add photos
- [ ] **Social media** — Instagram, Facebook pages
- [ ] **Local press** — Press release to Luton News, Beds on Sunday
- [ ] **Pre-launch offers:**
  - Founding member discounts
  - Free taster sessions
  - Refer-a-friend incentives
- [ ] **Signage** — External signage visible from road

### 5. Build Community (Before Opening)
- [ ] Create WhatsApp/Facebook group for interested players
- [ ] Partner with local tennis clubs for crossover
- [ ] Contact LTA for promotion support
- [ ] Reach out to local businesses for corporate bookings
- [ ] Plan launch event

---

## Launch Week

### 6. Soft Launch (Days 1-3)
- [ ] Open to friends, family, early sign-ups
- [ ] Test all systems under real conditions
- [ ] Gather feedback
- [ ] Fix any issues
- [ ] Staff shadowing and support

### 7. Grand Opening Event
- [ ] Set date (weekend preferred)
- [ ] Plan activities:
  - Free taster sessions
  - Exhibition match (local pro?)
  - Food/drinks
  - Prizes/giveaways
- [ ] Invite:
  - Local press
  - Council representatives
  - Local sports personalities
  - Business contacts
- [ ] Photographer/videographer for content
- [ ] Social media live coverage

### 8. First Month Operations
- [ ] Monitor bookings daily
- [ ] Respond to customer feedback quickly
- [ ] Adjust pricing/offers if needed
- [ ] Start coaching programmes
- [ ] Begin league/tournament planning
- [ ] Collect reviews (Google, Facebook)
