# Sign Lease

**Status:** ⏳ Not Started  
**Target:** Q2 2026  
**Dependencies:** 
- ✅ Property shortlist confirmed

## Objective

Secure a lease on the selected property with terms that protect the business.

## Tasks (Once Pre-Requisites Met)

### 1. Negotiate Heads of Terms
- [ ] Request draft Heads of Terms from landlord/agent
- [ ] **Key terms to negotiate:**
  - Rent amount (target <£45k/year for 4-court facility)
  - Rent-free period (ask for 3-6 months for fit-out)
  - Lease length (5-10 years typical; longer = more security)
  - Break clauses (3-year break gives flexibility)
  - Use clause (must allow Class E(d) indoor sport)
  - Alterations (right to install courts, lighting, etc.)
  - Subletting (may want flexibility for coaching space)
  - Dilapidations cap (limit liability at end of lease)
- [ ] Get landlord agreement on key terms before proceeding

### 2. Planning Pre-Application
- [ ] Submit pre-application to Luton Council
- [ ] Include: site plan, proposed use, operating hours
- [ ] **Budget:** £200-500 for pre-app advice
- [ ] Wait for written response (6-8 weeks typical)
- [ ] Review any conditions/concerns raised

See [Luton Planning Guide](../research/luton-planning-guide) for process details.

---

## Solicitor Required ⚖️

### 3. Lease Review
- [ ] **Engage commercial property solicitor**
- [ ] Provide Heads of Terms for review
- [ ] **Solicitor should check:**
  - Break clause conditions
  - Service charge caps
  - Repairing obligations (FRI vs internal only)
  - Permitted use aligns with padel/leisure
  - Assignment rights
  - Rent review mechanism (RPI cap recommended)
  - Guarantor requirements (avoid personal guarantees if possible)
- [ ] Negotiate any unfavourable terms
- [ ] Get final lease draft

**Budget:** £1.5-3k for lease review + negotiation

### 4. Complete Legal Due Diligence
- [ ] Solicitor to conduct title searches
- [ ] Check for restrictive covenants
- [ ] Verify landlord's ownership/right to lease
- [ ] Review any existing planning conditions on property

### 5. Sign Lease
- [ ] Final read-through of lease terms
- [ ] All partners review and agree
- [ ] Company to sign (not individuals)
- [ ] Pay deposit (typically 3-6 months rent)
- [ ] Exchange signed copies
- [ ] Obtain keys/access
