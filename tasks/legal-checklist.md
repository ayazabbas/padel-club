## Phase 1: Company Formation (Do Soon)

**Budget:** Â£1-3k

### Tasks
- [ ] **Form limited company (Ltd)**
  - Preferred over LLP for clearer liability
  - Register with Companies House
  - Appoint directors (all partners?)
  - Issue initial shares

- [ ] **Shareholder Agreement**
  - Equity splits between founders
  - Profit distribution schedule (quarterly recommended)
  - Decision-making process (reserved matters)
  - Exit mechanisms (buyback, sale, death/incapacity)
  - Deadlock resolution
  - Non-compete clauses
  - Drag-along/tag-along rights

- [ ] **Articles of Association**
  - May need bespoke version, not standard template

### Recommended Timing
Do this BEFORE approaching investors â€” they'll want to see a proper structure.

## Phase 2: Investor Agreements

**Budget:** Â£1-2k per investor

### Tasks
- [ ] **Term Sheet** (non-binding outline)
  - Investment amount
  - Equity percentage
  - Key rights and obligations

- [ ] **Investment Agreement** (Musharakah-compliant)
  - Profit-sharing ratios (must be percentage, not fixed return)
  - Loss-sharing provisions (proportional to capital)
  - No interest/riba clauses
  - Management rights
  - Information rights
  - Exit provisions
  - Dispute resolution

- [ ] **Updated Shareholder Agreement**
  - Incorporate new investor
  - Adjust voting thresholds if needed

### Islamic Finance Note
Standard investment templates won't work â€” solicitor needs to understand Musharakah structure or work with Islamic finance specialist.

## Phase 3: Lease Review

**Budget:** Â£1.5-3k

### Tasks
- [ ] **Review Heads of Terms**
  - Before agreeing anything in writing

- [ ] **Full Lease Review**
  - Rent and rent review mechanism
  - Break clause conditions (what triggers it?)
  - Repairing obligations (FRI = expensive)
  - Service charge caps
  - Permitted use (must allow Class E(d))
  - Alterations clause (can we install courts?)
  - Assignment and subletting
  - Guarantor requirements
  - Dilapidations liability

- [ ] **Negotiate Amendments**
  - Push back on unfavourable terms

- [ ] **Title Searches**
  - Verify landlord's ownership
  - Check for restrictive covenants

- [ ] **Complete & Exchange**
  - Sign in company name, not personal

## Recommended Solicitor Profile

Look for:
- Commercial property experience (for lease)
- Company/corporate experience (for formation, shareholder agreements)
- Ideally: familiarity with Islamic finance/Musharakah structures
- Local to Luton/Bedfordshire (convenient, knows local market)

### How to Find
- Law Society solicitor finder: [solicitors.lawsociety.org.uk](https://solicitors.lawsociety.org.uk/)
- Ask other local business owners for recommendations
- Check reviews on Google, Trustpilot

### Questions to Ask
1. Have you done commercial property leases before?
2. Can you handle company formation and shareholder agreements?
3. Are you familiar with Islamic finance structures (Musharakah)?
4. What are your fees? (Get fixed quotes where possible)
5. What's your typical turnaround time?
