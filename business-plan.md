## Competitor Analysis

### PadelPass Luton (at Venue360)

The only known padel facility in Luton.

- **Location:** Venue 360, 20 Gipsy Lane, Luton LU1 3JH
- **Courts:** 2 x panoramic courts
- **Cover:** 9.5m tall canopy (covered outdoor)
- **Pricing:** £32/hour (via Playtomic)
- **Hours:** 06:00 - 23:00 daily
- **Facilities:** Floodlit, café bar, beer garden, changing rooms, club shop, racket rental
- **Coaching:** Head Coach Charlie Attenborough, 8-week Padel Xpress course
- **Status:** Opened late 2025, memberships coming soon
- **Reviews:** Generally positive (Google reviews Oct 2025 - Jan 2026)

**Sources:**
- https://padelpass.co.uk/padel-pass-luton/
- https://playtomic.com/clubs/padel-pass-luton-pre-launch-packages-available-open-summer-2025

### Competitive Positioning

- PadelPass has first-mover advantage but only 2 courts
- Opportunity: Larger facility with more courts + additional amenities
- Opportunity: Different area of Luton with expansion room

## Court Costs

### ⚠️ Important Note

UK padel court suppliers do NOT publish prices online. All require site surveys and custom quotes. Costs vary significantly based on:
- Ground conditions and groundworks required
- Site access for delivery
- Court configuration (single vs multi-court)
- Cover/canopy specifications
- Lighting, drainage, utilities
