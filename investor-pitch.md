## The Opportunity

### Padel is Booming

- **Fastest-growing sport in the world** — 25 million players globally
- **UK growth:** 400+ courts (2020) → 2,000+ courts (2025), still massively undersupplied
- **Demand outstripping supply** — UK clubs averaging 65-71% occupancy
- **Premium pricing holds** — £32-50/hour across UK facilities

### Luton is Underserved

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Population | 225,000 |
| Existing Courts | 2 (PadelPass at Venue360) |
| Courts per Capita | 1:112,500 |
| National Average | 1:35,000 (and growing) |
| **Gap** | **4-6 courts needed just to match average** |

### Location Advantage

- M1 corridor (Junction 10-11) — excellent access
- 45 mins from London — attracts city commuters
- Strong local demand — diverse, young population
- Industrial units available at competitive rents
