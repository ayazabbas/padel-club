## Key Findings

- **Revenue:** UK courts generating £100-150k/year per court
- **Occupancy:** Indoor ~71%, outdoor ~45%
- **Break-even:** 18-24 months typical
- **Rent rule:** Should be <15% of revenue
- **Warning:** Hands-off approach = administration risk
