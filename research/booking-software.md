# Booking Software — Research

*Last Updated: 3 February 2026*

## Summary

- **Playtomic** is market leader (best reach, highest fees)
- **Court22** is cheaper alternative (1% + 35p per booking)
- Recommend: Start with Playtomic for visibility, review after year 1
- Budget: £3,000-5,000/year for platform fees

---

## Playtomic (Market Leader)

**Pros:**
- Largest player community
- Great for marketing and player acquisition
- Match finding, open plays, community features
- Used by Rocks Lane and many UK clubs

**Cons:**
- Fees can add up ($5,000+/year reported)
- Commission on every booking

**Website:** playtomic.com

---

## Court22 (Budget Alternative)

**Pros:**
- Much lower fees: 1% for clubs + 35p per player
- Clubs actively switching from Playtomic/MATCHi
- Growing platform

**Cons:**
- Smaller community than Playtomic
- Less marketing reach

**Website:** court22.com

---

## Recommendation

1. **Start with Playtomic** — leverage their community for initial visibility
2. **Review after 12 months** — assess if fees justify the acquisition value
3. **Consider Court22** as backup if fees too high
