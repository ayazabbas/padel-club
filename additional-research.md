# Padel Club Additional Research
*Last Updated: 3 February 2026*

## 1. Court Booking Software Options

### Playtomic (Market Leader)
- **Website:** playtomic.com
- **Features:** Court booking, match finding, open plays, player community
- **UK Presence:** Used by Rocks Lane and many other UK clubs
- **Pricing:** Contact for quote (typically commission-based)
- **Pros:** Largest community, great for player acquisition, marketing reach
- **Cons:** Fees can add up (one club reported $5,000+/year)

### MATCHi
- **Website:** matchi.se
- **Features:** Booking, match organization, membership management
- **UK Presence:** Growing, used by several UK clubs
- **User Feedback:** "Best interface" per Reddit users
- **Pricing:** Contact for quote

### Court22 (Newer Alternative)
- **Website:** court22.com
- **Features:** Booking platform with lower fees
- **Pricing:** 1% for clubs + 35 cents per player booking
- **Note:** Clubs switching from Playtomic/MATCHi due to lower fees

### PlayByPoint
- **Features:** Nice user interface, supports classes
- **User Feedback:** Praised for ease of booking and finding matches

### Key Considerations:
- Playtomic offers best player reach/marketing
- Lower-cost alternatives exist (Court22)
- Consider starting with Playtomic for visibility, review after 1 year
- Budget £3,000-5,000/year for booking platform fees

## 2. Insurance Requirements

### Mandatory Cover
- **Public Liability:** £5-15 million minimum (industry standard)
- **Employers' Liability:** £5 million minimum (legal requirement if you have employees)

### Recommended Cover
- **Professional Indemnity:** For coaching/instruction
- **Equipment Cover:** Courts, balls, rackets (up to £30k)
- **Building/Contents:** If responsible for property
- **Business Interruption:** Loss of income protection

### Key Providers
1. **LTA/Howden** - Official LTA partner for tennis/padel clubs
   - howdengroup.com/uk-en/lawn-tennis-association/clubs-and-venues
   
2. **Mann Broadbent** - Specialist in sport/leisure, 60+ years experience
   - mannbroadbent.co.uk (Padel-specific policies)
   
3. **Insure4Sport/Ripe** - Sports club insurance specialists
   - insure4sport.co.uk
   
4. **Protectivity** - Budget option from £3.62/month
   - protectivity.com

### Cost Estimate
- Small club (2 courts): £1,500-3,000/year
- Medium club (4+ courts): £3,000-6,000/year
- Note: Get multiple quotes, mention LTA registration for potential discounts
